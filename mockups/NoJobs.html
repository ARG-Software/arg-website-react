How we work

Four things we actually mean.

Most companies list values that could belong to a bank. Ours are operational — they decide what we ship, what we refuse, and who fits on the team.

01

Architecture first

The system is designed before it's written. If we can't explain it on a whiteboard, we don't build it yet.

NOT "ship fast, fix later"
02

No patchwork

If a fix needs a workaround, we refactor the abstraction. Band-aids compound; we'd rather pay the cost now.

NOT "good enough for now"
03

Production on a Tuesday

We deploy when we can stay close. We own what we ship — bugs, performance, the 3am page. No throw-it-over-the-wall.

NOT "merge Friday, ghost the weekend"
04

Direct, kind, no politics

Honest in code review, honest in retro. Small team — every personality compounds. We say the thing.

NOT "let's circle back offline"
