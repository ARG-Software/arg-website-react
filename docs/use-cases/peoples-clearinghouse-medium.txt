Services: Backend, Frontend Implementation, Mobile App Development, Payments Architecture, Interoperability, Live Demo Support

THE CHALLENGE

Mexico's community banking sector connects millions of rural people to financial services — but on infrastructure that can't interoperate with the broader payment network. People's Clearinghouse set out to change that: a community-owned payments ecosystem built on Mojaloop vNext, linking community banks, savings coops, and rural merchants to national-scale payment switching. The platform needed to handle 1,000–2,000 requests per second with sub-second transfers — and survive the most public test: a live demo at Interledger Summit 2025 in Mexico City, with rural merchants selling real goods to attendees through the app.

WHAT ARG DELIVERED

ARG extended years of Mojaloop vNext expertise into a purpose-built clearinghouse for Mexico's social financial sector — from payment API engineering to mobile app flows to live demo preparation.

- Designed PCH-specific switch and participant flows for community banks, savings coops, and regulated payment institutions
- Load-tested payment APIs under sustained cluster conditions, processing ~1,000–2,000 requests per second with linear scalability
- Tuned lookup and quote workflows to ~200–300ms end-to-end, with ledger transfers averaging ~770ms
- Validated reliability through multi-million request test runs with zero functional errors
- Built mobile app paths for account transfers, QR merchant payments, confirmations, and full transaction visibility
- Prepared the main-stage Interledger Summit demo and supported the live community showcase where rural merchants transacted with ~100 attendees without a single transfer failure

THE RESULTS

At Interledger Summit 2025, People's Clearinghouse proved it works — on stage, in front of a live audience, and immediately after in the hands of rural merchants selling coffee and traditional goods through the app. Zero transfer failures across ~1,000+ live transactions.

Key Metrics:
- Requests per second (sustained): ~2,000
- Average ledger transfer time: ~770ms
- Live showcase transfers (zero failures): 1,000+

The APIs proved they can support national-scale transaction switching — the foundation Mexico's community banking network needs to move from pilot to production.
